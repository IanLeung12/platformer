Iro And The Oblisks

This work in progress is a game inspired by ori and the will of the wisps*

By Me and Razor177 for ICS3U6 final project

current controls:

a,d: left and right

space: jump

Attack selection: 
1: Sword
2: Hammer
3: Bow
4: Rocket

left click to attack

shift: dash
